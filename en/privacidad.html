Privacy Policy

How we handle your information securely and ethically.

1. Introduction

At Biofactor, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or contact us through our forms.

2. Information Collection

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us via our Contact and Pharmacovigilance forms. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you choose to share with us.

3. Use of Information

The information we collect is used strictly for the purpose of responding to your inquiries, processing Pharmacovigilance reports in accordance with health regulations, and improving our services. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no transmission of data over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.
