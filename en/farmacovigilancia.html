The science dedicated to the detection, assessment, understanding and prevention of adverse effects of medicines.

Patient Care

Knowledge about the toxicity arising from the use of medicines draws particular attention from patients, prescribers, dispensers and regulatory authorities.

According to the WHO definition, Pharmacovigilance is the science and activities relating to the detection, assessment, understanding and prevention of adverse effects of medicines or any other medicine-related problem.

What is an Adverse Event (AE)?

Any untoward medical occurrence that may appear during treatment with a medicine, but which does not necessarily have a causal relationship with that treatment. In this case, there is a coincidence in time without any suspicion of a causal relationship.
